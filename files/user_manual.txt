Supported games with respective roles:
```valorant - duelist, initiator, controller, sentinel```
Generate random agent/hero:
```ply [game]
e.g. ply valorant```
Generate random agent/hero based on role:
```ply [game] [role]
e.g. ply valorant duelist```
Generate random agent/hero based on role (shorthand):
```ply [game] [role's initial]
e.g. ply valorant d```
Generate random 5 men agent/hero team:
```ply [game] 5men
e.g. ply valorant 5men```
Generate random 5 men agent/hero team (with custom roles):
```ply [game] 5men [number of roles in order]
e.g. ply valorant 5men 0122```
